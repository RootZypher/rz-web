Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Cancellation Window

You can cancel your order within 30 minutes of payment for a full refund. After 30 minutes, the service delivery process begins and cancellations may not be possible.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • ✅ Full Refund: If we fail to deliver service within 24 hours
  • ✅ Partial Refund: If you receive fewer followers/likes than ordered
  • ❌ No Refund: If service is delivered but you change your mind
  • ❌ No Refund: If Instagram removes followers due to policy violation

3. Trail Amount (₹5)

The ₹5 trail amount is non-refundable as it is used to verify payment method and enable auto-debit.

4. Refund Timeline

  • Refund requests processed within 3-5 business days
  • Money credited back in 5-7 business days
  • Refund goes to original payment method

5. How to Request Refund

Email us at refunds@instapremium.in with:

  • Order ID (found in success page)
  • Instagram username
  • Reason for refund
  • Screenshot of payment (optional)
